Mark-In

Final Year Project - Diploma in Information Technology (2016)

MarkIn, a time attendance system, developed using ASP.NET Web Apps and C#, is equipped with biometric and QR attendance-taking capabilities, on top of the basic operations required by the 3 target personas. Firstly, the Human Resource (HR) department of Singapore Polytechnic (SP) can efficiently monitor staff information and attendance, then use this information to carry out their day-to-day HR operations. Secondly, staffs of SP can take attendance using their biometric, review their attendance and manage events that require attendance taking. Thirdly, members of the public can register and participate events at ease with the use of QR code for attendance taking and contact exchange.
