MySIM

Final Year Project - Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Computing and Information Systems (2020)

MySIM, a university application built with Xamarin Forms and C#, is equipped with an AI chatbot on top of the essential day-to-day operations required by a student in the Singapore Institute of Management (SIM). It offers portability and convenience to the students in carrying out their day-to-day activities such as checking their class schedule, taking attendance, viewing their virtual student card, and seeking any academic support. The administrators can also use this application to manage this information.
